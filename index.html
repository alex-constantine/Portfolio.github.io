Recent Work

3D CAD Model of Prosthetic Limb

This is a 3D model designed and assembled in Siemens NX. The project involved 30+ hours of effective teamwork and constant communication. It involved concept generation, initial research and an entire design process. We considered many factors in our design; movement, materials and compactness.

Engineering Drawings of Prosthetic Limb

The final submission required a complete engineering drawing package ready for manufacturing. I had to include an engineering drawing for every component, the assembly drawings and exploded view drawing. A parts list was also necessary for the model to be ready for manufacture.

Engineering Drawings of Prosthetic Limb

These are the movement drawings produced as required in the project brief. I utilised Siemens NX to then create actual movement of the fingers and wrist to be included in our presentation. In our design we created space for our movement mechanisms to exist as well as the electrical components.

Wearable Medical Device

This is a 3D rendered CAD model of the wearable medical device produced. The project aims were to use PPG signals to display critical physiological parameters. A group presentation was required to explain how the device works including software and hardware. We also developed an app to present our idea.

3D Bioscaffold CAD Model

This project required production of Triply Periodic Minimal Surfaces (TMPS) to develop structures with pore morphology. Reasoning behind the choice of design and materials, as well as all the characterisation performed on the scaffolds, and physical samples of the 3D printed scaffolds were requested.

Electrical Eye System

Initially, a digital circuit was designed using MultiSim. We then used our digital circuit blueprint to create a real system to detect light disturbances using photodiodes and other hardware. The project also required a presentation giving an explanation of the hardware and a circuit demonstration.
